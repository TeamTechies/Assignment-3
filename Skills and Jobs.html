Skills and Jobs

These skilled roles would include a creative director who would be able to envision the future of the magic mirror. It would be important for them to get the fundamental look of the mirror to match the aesthetic that the creative visionaries saw, while also achieving the goal of squeezing the mirror, LCD screen and Raspberry Pi technology into a slim low-profile design.
